Common Questions

Everything you
need to know

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings we recommend 12–18 months. For birthdays and baby showers, 3–6 months is ideal — though we can work with shorter timelines where availability allows.

Do you work outside Melbourne?

Absolutely. We regularly work across the Yarra Valley, Mornington Peninsula, and regional Victoria. Travel fees may apply beyond 60km.

Can I customise a package?

Every event we plan is bespoke. Our packages are starting points — we tailor everything around your vision, guest numbers, and budget.

What's included in day-of coordination?

We arrive during setup, manage all vendors, keep your timeline on track, and stay until the event is complete — so you don't need to think about a thing.

Do you have preferred vendors?

We have a trusted network of Melbourne's finest photographers, florists, caterers and venues — but we're equally happy to work with suppliers you love.

How do I get started?

Simply reach out via our contact page or call us directly. We'll arrange a complimentary discovery call to talk through your event.
